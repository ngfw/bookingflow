## General Questions

### What is the BookingFlow?
The BookingFlow is a comprehensive software solution designed specifically for service businesss, spas, and wellness centers. It helps manage appointments, clients, staff, inventory, payments, and business operations all in one integrated platform.

### Who can use this system?
The system is designed for:
- **Service-based businesses** of all sizes
- **Hair salons** and barbershops
- **Nail salons** and spas
- **Wellness centers** and massage therapy clinics
- **Multi-location** salon chains
- **Franchise operations**

### What are the main features?
Key features include:
- Online appointment booking
- Client management and profiles
- Staff scheduling and management
- Inventory tracking
- Payment processing
- Reporting and analytics
- Loyalty programs
- Marketing tools
- Multi-location support

### Is there a mobile app?
Yes, the system includes a mobile app for both iOS and Android devices. The app allows clients to book appointments, view their history, and manage their accounts on the go.

### How much does it cost?
Pricing varies based on the number of users, locations, and features needed. Contact our sales team for a customized quote based on your specific requirements.

### Is there a free trial?
Yes, we offer a 30-day free trial with full access to all features. No credit card required to start your trial.

### What support is available?
We provide comprehensive support including:
- Email support
- Phone support
- Live chat
- Video tutorials
- Documentation
- Training materials

## Account and Login

### How do I create an account?
1. Visit the registration page
2. Enter your email address and password
3. Complete your profile information
4. Verify your email address
5. Log in to access the system

### I forgot my password. How do I reset it?
1. Click "Forgot Password" on the login page
2. Enter your email address
3. Check your email for reset instructions
4. Follow the link to create a new password

### Why can't I log in?
Common login issues include:
- Incorrect email or password
- Account not verified
- Account locked due to multiple failed attempts
- Browser cache issues
- Network connectivity problems

### How do I change my password?
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "My Profile" or "Account Settings"
3. Click "Change Password"
4. Enter your current password
5. Enter and confirm your new password

### Can I have multiple user accounts?
Yes, you can have multiple user accounts with different roles and permissions. Contact your administrator to create additional accounts.

### What user roles are available?
The system supports three main roles:
- **Client**: Can book appointments and manage their profile
- **Staff**: Can manage appointments, clients, and process payments
- **Administrator**: Full system access and configuration

## Appointment Booking

### How do I book an appointment?
1. Log in to your account
2. Click "Book Appointment"
3. Select your desired service
4. Choose your preferred staff member
5. Pick a date and time
6. Add any special requests
7. Confirm your booking

### Can I book appointments for other people?
Yes, if you have the appropriate permissions. You can book appointments for family members or clients if you're a staff member.

### How far in advance can I book?
You can typically book appointments up to 30 days in advance, depending on your salon's settings.

### What if I need to cancel or reschedule?
1. Go to "My Appointments"
2. Find the appointment you want to change
3. Click "Reschedule" or "Cancel"
4. Follow the prompts to complete the change

### Is there a cancellation policy?
Yes, each salon sets their own cancellation policy. Check with your salon for specific terms and any fees that may apply.

### Can I book recurring appointments?
Yes, you can set up recurring appointments for regular services. This feature is available for clients with established service patterns.

### What if my preferred time isn't available?
The system will show you alternative times that are available. You can also:
- Choose a different staff member
- Select a different date
- Join a waitlist for your preferred time
- Contact the salon directly

### How do I join a waitlist?
If your preferred time isn't available, you can join a waitlist. You'll be notified if a cancellation occurs and the time becomes available.

## Payments and Billing

### What payment methods are accepted?
The system accepts:
- Credit and debit cards
- PayPal
- Apple Pay
- Google Pay
- Cash (for in-person payments)
- Gift cards
- Store credit

### Is my payment information secure?
Yes, all payment information is encrypted and processed through secure payment gateways. We never store your full credit card information.

### Can I pay in advance?
Yes, you can pay for services in advance when booking online or through the mobile app.

### How do I view my payment history?
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "Payment History" or "Billing"
3. View all past payments and receipts
4. Download receipts as needed

### What if I need a refund?
Contact your salon directly to request a refund. Refunds are processed according to the salon's refund policy.

### Can I use a gift card?
Yes, gift cards can be used for services and products. Enter your gift card code during checkout.

### How do I add store credit?
Store credit is typically added by salon staff for refunds, promotions, or loyalty rewards. Contact your salon to add store credit to your account.

### Are there any processing fees?
Processing fees vary by payment method and are typically 2-9% of the transaction amount. Check with your salon for specific fee information.

## Inventory and Products

### How do I track product usage?
1. Go to "Inventory" or "Products"
2. Select the products used during a service
3. Enter the quantity used
4. The system automatically updates inventory levels

### What happens when inventory is low?
The system sends automatic alerts when inventory levels fall below the minimum threshold. You can then reorder products as needed.

### Can I track product expiration dates?
Yes, the system tracks expiration dates and sends alerts when products are nearing expiration.

### How do I add new products?
1. Go to "Inventory Management"
2. Click "Add Product"
3. Enter product details
4. Set minimum stock levels
5. Add supplier information

### Can I track product costs?
Yes, you can track:
- Purchase costs
- Usage costs
- Profit margins
- Cost per service

### How do I manage suppliers?
1. Go to "Suppliers" or "Vendors"
2. Add supplier information
3. Track purchase history
4. Manage reorder processes
