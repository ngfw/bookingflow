## Getting Started

### System Requirements
- Modern web browser (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Internet connection
- JavaScript enabled

### Accessing the System
1. Open your web browser
2. Navigate to your salon's website
3. Click "Book Appointment" or "Client Portal"

---

## Creating Your Account

### Step 1: Registration
1. Click "Register" or "Sign Up"
2. Fill in your information:
   - Full Name
   - Email Address
   - Phone Number
   - Password
   - Confirm Password
3. Click "Create Account"

### Step 2: Email Verification
1. Check your email for a verification link
2. Click the verification link
3. Your account will be activated

### Step 3: Complete Your Profile
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "My Profile"
3. Add additional information:
   - Date of Birth
   - Address
   - Emergency Contact
   - Service Preferences
   - Allergies or Medical Conditions

## Booking Appointments

### Online Booking Process

#### Step 1: Select Service
1. Click "Book Appointment"
2. Browse available services
3. Click on your desired service
4. Review service details, duration, and price

#### Step 2: Choose Staff Member
1. Select your preferred staff member
2. View staff profiles and specialties
3. Choose "Any Available" if you have no preference

#### Step 3: Select Date and Time
1. Choose your preferred date
2. View available time slots
3. Select a time that works for you
4. Green slots = Available
5. Red slots = Unavailable

#### Step 4: Add Special Requests
1. Add any special requests or notes
2. Mention allergies or sensitivities
3. Request specific products or techniques

#### Step 5: Confirm Booking
1. Review your appointment details
2. Confirm your contact information
3. Click "Book Appointment"
4. You'll receive a confirmation email

### Booking Tips
- Book in advance for popular time slots
- Check for seasonal promotions
- Consider package deals for multiple services
- Book recurring appointments for convenience

## Managing Your Appointments

### Viewing Your Appointments
1. Log in to your account
2. Go to "My Appointments"
3. View upcoming and past appointments
4. See appointment status and details

### Rescheduling Appointments
1. Find your appointment in "My Appointments"
2. Click "Reschedule"
3. Choose a new date and time
4. Confirm the changes
5. You'll receive an updated confirmation

### Canceling Appointments
1. Find your appointment in "My Appointments"
2. Click "Cancel"
3. Select a reason for cancellation
4. Confirm the cancellation
5. Check the cancellation policy for any fees

### Appointment Reminders
- Email reminders sent 24 hours before
- SMS reminders sent 2 hours before
- Push notifications if using mobile app
